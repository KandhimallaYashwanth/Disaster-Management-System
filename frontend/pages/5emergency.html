Emergency Contacts

Important emergency contact numbers for immediate assistance during disasters and emergency situations.

Medical Emergency

108

For medical emergencies, ambulance services, and immediate medical assistance.

Fire Emergency

101

For fire emergencies, rescue operations, and fire hazard reporting.

Police

100

For police assistance, reporting crimes, and security emergencies.

National Emergency Helpline

112

Single number for all emergency services (police, fire, ambulance).

Disaster Management Authority

1078

National Disaster Management Authority helpline for disaster-related assistance.

Child Helpline

1098

Emergency helpline for children in distress, especially during disasters.

Blood Bank

1910

For emergency blood requirements during disasters.

Flood Control Room

1077

District-level flood control room for flood-related emergencies.

During an Emergency:

  • Stay calm and provide clear information about your emergency
  • Share your exact location with landmarks if possible
  • Follow instructions provided by emergency personnel
  • Keep emergency numbers saved in your phone and written down in your home
  • If in doubt, call the National Emergency Helpline at 112
  • Try to provide information about the number of people affected
